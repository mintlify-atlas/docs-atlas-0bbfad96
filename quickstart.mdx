## Download and create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Chapter from the App Store">
    Chapter is available for iPhone running iOS 16 or later. Search for "Chapter" in the App Store or visit [chapteruni.com](https://chapteruni.com) to download.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your email address. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.
    
    <Note>
      Your email will never be shared with third parties. Chapter uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    Add your basic information:
    - First and last name
    - Date of birth
    - School or sixth form (optional but recommended for better matching)
    
    You can customize your profile later with a photo, bio, and social media links.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Complete your onboarding

Chapter's onboarding flow helps us understand your situation and goals to provide personalized recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell us your enrollment status">
    Select which best describes you:
    
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="I'm still browsing courses">
        **Prospect status** - Perfect if you're in Year 10-13 and exploring your options. Chapter will show you course discovery tools, career guidance, and university information.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="I know which course I'm taking">
        **Offer Holder status** - You've received offers or made your decision. Chapter will help you prepare for university with accommodation search, checklists, and preparation guides.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="I'm already at uni">
        **Enrolled status** - You're currently studying. Chapter becomes your campus companion with timetables, societies, events, and social features.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your grades (prospects only)">
    If you're exploring courses, add your predicted or achieved grades:
    
    - Select your qualification (A-Levels, BTECs, Scottish Highers, IB, etc.)
    - Add each subject and grade
    - Chapter automatically calculates your UCAS points
    
    This helps Chapter Match recommend courses that match your academic profile.
    
    <Tip>
      You can update your grades anytime in Settings → Profile → Academic Information
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your preferences">
    Help Chapter understand what matters to you:
    
    **Location preferences:**
    - Choose regions you're willing to study in (London, South East, Scotland, etc.)
    - Set your maximum distance from home (or select "anywhere in the UK")
    - Add your home postcode for accurate distance calculations
    
    **Interests and sports:**
    - Select your interests (programming, entrepreneurship, nature, politics, etc.)
    - Choose sports you'd like to continue at university
    
    These help Chapter recommend universities with societies and facilities that match your lifestyle.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Prioritize what matters">
    Rank these factors by importance to you:
    
    **For university life:**
    - Accommodation quality
    - Cost of living
    - Diversity
    - Nightlife and partying
    - Safety
    - Sports facilities
    - Academic reputation
    
    **For courses:**
    - Graduate employment prospects
    - Graduate salaries
    - Student satisfaction (NSS scores)
    - Subject rankings
    - University rankings
    - Sandwich placements available
    - Location
    - Cost
    
    Chapter uses these priorities to personalize your search results and comparisons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Start exploring courses

Once you've completed onboarding, you're ready to discover your perfect course.

### Option 1: Use Chapter Match (recommended)

Chapter Match is an AI-powered tool that finds courses matching your profile, grades, and preferences.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Chapter Match">
    From your Hub, tap the **Chapter Match** widget, or go to the Search tab and tap **Start Matching**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Swipe through course recommendations">
    Chapter presents courses one at a time, tailored to your grades and preferences.
    
    - **Swipe right** or tap the heart to save courses you're interested in
    - **Swipe left** to skip courses that don't appeal to you
    - Tap a course card to view full details before deciding
    
    The more you swipe, the better Chapter understands your preferences and improves recommendations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your matches">
    Courses you liked are automatically saved to your shortlist. Access them anytime from:
    
    - Hub → **Course Shortlist** widget
    - Lists tab → **My Lists**
    - Chapter Match → **View Matches** button
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option 2: Browse and search

Prefer to explore on your own terms? Use Chapter's powerful search and filtering.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Search tab">
    Tap **Search** in the bottom navigation to access course and university search.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose what to explore">
    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Courses" icon="graduation-cap">
        Browse 37,000+ courses by subject, university, or keyword
      </Card>
      <Card title="Universities" icon="building-columns">
        Explore 150+ UK universities with detailed profiles
      </Card>
      <Card title="Careers" icon="briefcase">
        Discover career paths and see which courses lead to them
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter your search">
    Narrow down results using powerful filters:
    
    **For courses:**
    - Subject area (Business, Engineering, Medicine, etc.)
    - UCAS points required
    - Course length (3 years, 4 years with placement, etc.)
    - Qualification (BSc, BA, MEng, etc.)
    - Location and distance from home
    - Tuition fees
    
    **For universities:**
    - Region
    - University type (Russell Group, modern, specialist)
    - Student population size
    - Accommodation guarantee
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compare your favorites">
    Found multiple courses or universities you like? Use the **Compare** feature:
    
    1. Tap **Compare** in the Hub or Search tab
    2. Select 2-4 courses or universities
    3. See side-by-side comparisons of rankings, costs, graduate outcomes, and more
    
    <img
      src="/images/compare-courses.png"
      alt="Compare courses side-by-side"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Build your shortlist

As you explore, save courses to your shortlist for easy access later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a shortlist">
    From the Lists tab, tap **Create New List** and choose:
    - **Course List** - For saving degree programs
    - **University List** - For saving institutions
    - **Accommodation List** - For saving housing options (offer holders)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add items to your list">
    When viewing any course, university, or accommodation, tap the **Add to List** button and select your shortlist.
    
    <Tip>
      You can create multiple lists, such as "Top Choices," "Backup Options," or organized by subject.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View and manage your shortlists">
    Access your lists anytime from:
    - **Lists tab** - See all your saved items
    - **Hub** - Use the Course Shortlist widget for quick access
    
    From your list, you can:
    - Swipe through courses in full-screen view
    - Compare items in your list
    - Share your list with parents, teachers, or friends
    - Mark courses as firm, insurance, or additional choices
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Explore universities and courses in detail

### University profiles

Tap any university to view:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Overview" icon="info-circle">
    - Key statistics (student population, acceptance rate, etc.)
    - Campus location and map
    - University rankings (Guardian, Times, Complete University Guide)
    - Student satisfaction scores
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Courses" icon="book">
    - All courses offered at this university
    - Filter by subject area
    - Sort by ranking, entry requirements, or graduate salary
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Student life" icon="users">
    - Accommodation options and guarantees
    - Societies and sports clubs
    - Nightlife and social scene ratings
    - Cost of living estimates
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Campus" icon="map-location-dot">
    - Interactive campus map
    - Photos and virtual tours
    - Transport links and accessibility
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Tap any course to view:

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Modules" icon="list">

  <Accordion title="Outcomes" icon="chart-line">

    - Average graduate salary

  <Accordion title="Student reviews" icon="star">

    - Teaching quality ratings
    - Course organization ratings
