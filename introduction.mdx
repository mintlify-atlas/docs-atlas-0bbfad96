## What is Chapter?

Chapter is a comprehensive iOS app designed exclusively for UK students, guiding you through every stage of your higher education journey. From exploring courses and universities in Year 10 to managing your campus life as a student, Chapter provides personalized tools, insights, and connections to help you make informed decisions and thrive at university.

<Note>
  Chapter adapts to your journey stage, showing you relevant features and information based on whether you're exploring courses, holding an offer, or already enrolled.
</Note>

## Your personalized hub

The Chapter Hub is your command center, featuring customizable widgets that give you quick access to the features you use most. Drag, drop, and arrange widgets to create your perfect dashboard.

<img
  src="/images/hub-widgets.png"
  alt="Chapter Hub with customizable widgets"
/>

Available widgets include:
- **Chapter Match** - Continue your course matching journey
- **Leaderboards** - Quick access to course and university rankings
- **Compare** - Jump into comparing courses, universities, or apprenticeships
- **Course shortlist** - View and manage your saved courses
- **Career paths** - Explore career sectors and roles
- **UCAS Journey** - Track your application progress
- **What's Next** - See personalized recommendations for your next steps
- And many more...
