## Stage 1: Prospect

**"I'm still browsing courses"**

You're exploring your options, researching courses and universities, and building your shortlist. This stage typically includes Year 10-13 students, gap year students, or anyone considering university.

### What Chapter shows you

#### Hub widgets available

Your personalized hub includes widgets designed for course discovery:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Chapter Match" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" defaultOpen>
    AI-powered course matching based on your grades, interests, and preferences. Swipe through personalized recommendations to find courses that fit your profile.
    
    **How it works:**
    1. Chapter analyzes your predicted/achieved grades
    2. Considers your location preferences and maximum distance
    3. Factors in your interests and sports
    4. Ranks results by your stated priorities (cost, grad prospects, etc.)
    5. Presents courses one at a time for you to like or skip
    
    The more you swipe, the smarter recommendations become.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Career Paths" icon="road">
    Explore career sectors and individual roles to understand what you might want to do after graduation. Each career shows:
    - Role descriptions and typical responsibilities
    - Salary expectations (entry-level to experienced)
    - Required qualifications and skills
    - Related degree subjects
    - Job market outlook
    
    Perfect for students who want to choose their course based on career goals.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Course Shortlist" icon="heart">
    Quick access to your saved courses. View them as:
    - **List view** - See all courses at a glance
    - **Carousel view** - Swipe through courses full-screen
    - **Compare view** - See side-by-side comparisons
    
    You can create multiple shortlists (e.g., "Top 5," "Backup Options," "Engineering Courses").
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compare" icon="scale-balanced">
    Jump straight into comparing:
    - **Courses** - Entry requirements, rankings, graduate salaries, modules
    - **Universities** - Rankings, student satisfaction, costs, facilities
    - **Apprenticeships** - Salary while learning, qualifications earned, employer reputations
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Leaderboards" icon="trophy">
    See how courses and universities rank across metrics that matter to you:
    - **Course leaderboards** - Filter by subject (Accounting, Computer Science, etc.)
    - **University leaderboards** - Overall, by region, or by specific criteria
    
    Sort by graduate prospects, student satisfaction, research quality, or overall ranking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Career Arcade" icon="gamepad">
    Make career exploration fun with interactive games:
    - **Aim & Learn** - Test your knowledge about different careers
    - **Quick Think** - Rapid-fire career matching challenges
    - **Day in the Life** - Experience typical workdays in various professions
    
    Compete on leaderboards and earn achievements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cost Estimator" icon="calculator">
    Calculate the true cost of university for each course on your shortlist:
    - Tuition fees (per year and total)
    - Accommodation costs (by housing type)
    - Living expenses (food, transport, entertainment)
    - Course-specific costs (lab fees, equipment, placements)
    - Total cost vs student finance available
    
    Compare costs across different universities and cities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's Next" icon="compass-drafting">
    Personalized recommendations for your next steps:
    - Upcoming open days for universities on your shortlist
    - Suggested courses based on your browsing history
    - Career paths aligned with your interests
    - Application deadlines approaching
    - Tasks to complete in your UCAS application
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stage 2: Offer Holder

**"I know which course I'm taking"**

You've received offers or made your firm choice. Chapter now focuses on helping you prepare for university, compare your final options, and make informed decisions.

### What changes

When you update your status to Offer Holder, Chapter transforms:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your course shortlist">
    Add up to 5 courses (matching UCAS choices):
    - Mark one as **Firm** (your first choice)
    - Mark one as **Insurance** (your backup)
    - Mark others as **Additional** choices
    
    Chapter uses this to show you relevant accommodation, societies, and events for your chosen universities.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your planned start year">
    Tell Chapter when you plan to start university (e.g., September 2026). This helps with:
    - Timeline planning and deadline tracking
    - Age-appropriate event recommendations
    - Connecting you with students in the same intake
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your hub adapts">
    New widgets appear, focused on preparation:
    - **UCAS Journey** - Track application stages and results
    - **Accommodation Finder** - Browse housing at your firm choice uni
    - **Preparation Checklist** - Tasks to complete before arriving
    - **Student Finance Guide** - Apply for funding
    - **What's Next** - Personalized next steps for offer holders
  </Step>
</Steps>

    - ✅ Buy essentials for halls

  <Accordion title="Course Comparison" icon="scale-balanced">

## Stage 3: Enrolled

**"I'm already at uni"**

You're now a university student! Chapter becomes your campus companion, helping you manage lectures, connect with coursemates, and make the most of student life.

### What changes

When you update to Enrolled status:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your university">
    Add your university and course to unlock:
    - University-specific campus feed
    - Group chats for your course, accommodation, and societies
    - Events and society meetups
    - Lecture and exam timetables (if your uni integrates)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your year of study">
    Select whether you're a:
    - Fresher (1st year)
    - 2nd year
    - 3rd year
    - Final year
    - On placement
    - On year abroad
    - Foundation year
    
    This personalizes your feed and connects you with students in the same year.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join groups">
    Chapter creates/finds groups for:
    - Your course (e.g., "BSc Computer Science 2026")
    - Your accommodation (e.g., "Liberty Point Flat 3B")
    - Societies you've joined
    - Sports teams
    - Subject modules
    - Social groups and friend circles
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your stage

Your enrollment status isn't permanent - update it as your situation changes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Tap your profile picture → **Settings**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select 'Profile'">
    Navigate to **Profile** → **Enrollment Status**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your new status">
    Select:
    - Prospect
    - Offer Holder
    - Enrolled
    - On leave of absence
    - Graduated
    - Withdrawn
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update additional details">
    Depending on your new status, you may be asked to:
    - Add or remove your university
    - Set your course
    - Update your year of study
    - Add your planned start date
  </Step>
</Steps>

Chapter will immediately adjust to show relevant features for your new stage.

<Warning>
  Changing your enrollment status will affect which widgets, tabs, and features you see. Some data (like shortlists and messages) persists across stages, but others (like timetables) are stage-specific.
</Warning>

## Tips for each stage

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Prospects">
    **Make the most of the discovery phase:**
    
    ✅ Use Chapter Match to discover courses you wouldn't have found through traditional search
    
    ✅ Don't just focus on course content - explore university life, societies, and locations
    
    ✅ Use the Cost Estimator early to understand financial implications
    
    ✅ Explore careers before committing to a subject - make sure your degree leads somewhere you want to go
    
    ✅ Attend open days (use Chapter to find and track them) - nothing beats visiting in person
    
    ✅ Create multiple shortlists to organize your options (e.g., "Dream Choices," "Realistic Options," "Safety Schools")
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Offer Holders">
    **Prepare thoroughly before arriving:**
    
    ✅ Complete the Preparation Checklist - don't leave things to the last minute
    
    ✅ Research accommodation carefully - where you live affects your entire uni experience
    
    ✅ Join society mailing lists early to get first dibs on popular clubs
    
    ✅ Connect with future coursemates in your course group chat
    
    ✅ Familiarize yourself with campus using the interactive map
    
    ✅ Apply for student finance ASAP - it can take weeks to process
    
    ✅ Use the Student Finance Guide to understand your loan, grants, and bursaries
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enrolled Students">
    **Thrive at university:**
    
    ✅ Join groups early - your course chat is the best place to make friends and get help
    
    ✅ Try new societies in Freshers - you can always leave if they're not for you
    
    ✅ Use the Campus Feed to discover events, buy textbooks, and find opportunities
    
    ✅ Keep your timetable updated - missing lectures is easier than you think
    
    ✅ Customize notification settings so you don't get overwhelmed by group chats
    
    ✅ Engage with your flat chat - these people are your support network for the year
    
    ✅ Use What's On to discover events outside your usual circles
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
