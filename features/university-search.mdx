University Search allows students to explore detailed profiles of 140+ UK universities, compare institutions, and discover the best fit for their higher education journey.

## Search & Browse

Multiple ways to discover universities:

### Search Bar

Intelligent search supporting:
- Full university names (e.g., "University of Oxford")
- Abbreviations (e.g., "UCL", "LSE")
- Nicknames (e.g., "Oxbridge")
- Location (e.g., "universities in London")

## University Profile

Each university profile features a multi-tab interface with comprehensive information:

### Profile Tab

Key university statistics and overview:

#### Quick Stats Bar

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Demographics">
    - Total student population
    - International student percentage
    - Female to male ratio
    - Student to staff ratio
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Academic">
    - Average UCAS entry points
    - Degree completion rate
    - Russell Group membership
    - Founded year
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Location">
    - Distance from your location
    - City population size
    - Campus vs. city university
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

#### University Description

Official university overview including:
- History and heritage
- Academic strengths
- Notable alumni
- Research focus areas
- Campus facilities

<Note>
  Descriptions are pulled from verified university sources and updated annually.
</Note>

#### Campus Locations

Interactive map showing:
- Main campus location
- Satellite campuses
- Distance to city center
- Tap to expand full-screen map view

Detailed analytics and rankings:

#### National Rankings

### Graphs Tab

Visual analytics and comparisons:

#### Radar Chart

Spider graph comparing 6 key metrics:
- Accommodation quality
- Cost affordability
- Diversity score
- Nightlife quality
- Safety rating
- Sports facilities

The larger the shape, the better the university performs across these dimensions.

#### Graduate Outcomes

Interactive charts showing:
- Employment rate 15 months after graduation
- Average graduate starting salary
- Further study percentage
- Sector distribution of graduates

#### Demographics Breakdown

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Student Origins">
    Pie chart of UK vs. international students with country breakdown
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Age Distribution">
    Graph showing undergraduate vs. postgraduate vs. mature students
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Subject Popularity">
    Bar chart of largest departments by enrollment
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Courses Tab

Integrated course search filtered to this university:
- All courses offered at this institution
- Search and filter by subject, entry requirements, fees
- Quick action to shortlist or compare courses
- Tap any course to view full course details

<Info>
  Course search within university profiles uses the same powerful filtering system as the main Course Discovery feature.
</Info>

### Open Days Tab

Upcoming open day events:
- In-person campus visits
- Virtual open days
- Subject-specific taster days
- Applicant visit days (for offer holders)

Filter by:
- Date range
- Event type (virtual/in-person)
- Subject area

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Open Days">
    View chronological list of upcoming events
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Calendar">
    Tap "Add to Calendar" to set reminders
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Direct link to university registration page
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Accommodation Tab

Student housing options at this university:

#### University Halls

Official university accommodation showing:
- Hall names and locations
- Room types (ensuite, shared bathroom, studio)
- Prices per week
- Catering options
- Distance from main campus

#### Private Accommodation

Third-party student housing near campus:
- Provider name (e.g., Unite Students, Vita Student)
- Property types and amenities
- Price comparison
- Distance to campus

<Note>
  Accommodation data is pulled from Chapter's accommodation database with 2,500+ verified properties.
</Note>

### Societies Tab

Student societies and clubs at this university:
- Full list of registered societies (20,000+ across all universities)
- Filter by category: Academic, Sports, Cultural, Religious, Special Interest
- Member counts and activity levels
- Contact information and social media links

<Tip>
  Use the search bar to find specific interest groups (e.g., "debate", "photography", "sustainability").
</Tip>

## Interactive Features

### Bottom Action Bar

Persistent action buttons at the bottom of every profile:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Website" icon="globe">
    In-app browser to official university site
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compare" icon="columns">
    Add to university comparison tool
  </Card>
  <Card title="Like/Dislike" icon="heart">
    Swipe-style interactions to shortlist universities
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### User Interactions

Track your university preferences:
- **Likes**: Universities you're interested in (saved to profile)
- **Dislikes**: Universities to hide from recommendations
- **Views**: Recently viewed universities (up to 100)
- **Shortlists**: Custom lists for UCAS choices, backup options, etc.

## Similar Universities

Intelligent recommendations based on:
- Geographic proximity
- Similar entry requirements
- Comparable rankings
- Subject strengths overlap
- Student demographics similarity

## University Comparison

Tap "Compare" to add universities to side-by-side comparison:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Info">
    - Entry requirements
    - Student numbers
    - Key facts
    - Facilities
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stats">
    - All rankings side-by-side
    - Color-coded best performers
    - Percentage differences
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Analysis">
    - AI-generated comparison summary
    - Strengths and weaknesses
    - Best fit recommendations
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  You can compare up to 4 universities at once. Remove universities by tapping the X button.
</Warning>

## Map View

See universities on an interactive map:
- University markers with logos
- Cluster markers showing multiple universities in a region
- Filter by Russell Group, location, or ranking tier
- Tap markers to view quick stats popup
- Switch between satellite and standard map styles
