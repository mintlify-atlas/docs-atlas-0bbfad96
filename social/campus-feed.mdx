## Overview

The Campus Feed is your personalized window into university life. See posts from friends, groups, societies, and official university channels—all in one intelligent feed that adapts to your journey through higher education.

<Info>
Your feed is personalized based on your university, year of study, interests, and the groups you've joined.
</Info>

## Feed Tabs

Swipe between three intelligent feed modes:

### For You
Personalized content curated specifically for you based on:
- Your university and course
- Your current stage (exploring, applying, offer holder, fresher, etc.)
- Groups you've joined
- Societies and sports you follow
- Your engagement history

### Explore
Discover what's trending across your university:
- Popular posts with high engagement
- Upcoming events
- Society highlights
- Campus news and announcements
- Content from across all groups

### Following
See posts only from:
- Your friends
- Groups you've joined
- Societies and sports teams you follow
- Accounts you specifically follow

<Tip>
Switch tabs with a single tap. The tab indicator shows which feed you're viewing.
</Tip>

## Content Types

The Campus Feed displays a variety of content tailored to student life:

### User Posts
Students can share:
- Text updates
- Photos and images
- Thoughts and experiences
- Questions for the community
- Recommendations and tips

**Engagement Options:**
- Like posts
- Comment and reply
- Share to groups
- Save for later

### Confessions
Anonymous posts from students at your university. Each confession is posted by a unique alias like "🦊 Anonymous Fox" or "🐨 Anonymous Koala".

**Features:**
- Complete anonymity
- University-specific (only see confessions from your uni)
- Like and comment anonymously
- Report inappropriate content

<Warning>
Confessions are anonymous but must follow community guidelines. Bullying, harassment, or hateful content will be removed.
</Warning>

### Events
Upcoming events from:
- Student Union
- Societies and sports clubs
- University official events
- Student-created events

**Event Cards Show:**
- Event name and cover image
- Date, time, and location
- Organizer information
- Attendee count
- Ticket price (if applicable)
- Quick RSVP button

### Open Days
Discovery tool for prospective students:
- University open day listings
- Virtual and in-person events
- Department-specific sessions
- Booking information and links
- Spots remaining indicator

**Targeted at:** Exploring and Applying stages

### Society Updates
News and announcements from university societies:
- Event announcements
- Membership drives
- Achievement celebrations
- Meeting reminders
- Social media highlights

### Accommodation Alerts
Timely housing information:
- **Price changes**: "Price drop at Liberty Living!"
- **Availability updates**: "12 rooms now available"
- **New listings**: Featured accommodation providers
- **Special offers**: Early bird discounts, group deals

**Includes:**
- Current and previous pricing
- Available room count
- Direct booking links
- Provider photos

### Freshers Tips
Curated advice for new students:
- Verified tips from the Chapter team
- Student-submitted advice
- Packing lists
- Budgeting guidance
- Social tips
- Academic advice

**Categories:**
- Freshers week
- Moving in
- Making friends
- Academic success
- Student life hacks

### Polls
Community polls from groups and societies:
- Multiple choice questions
- Real-time voting results
- Vote counts and percentages
- Expiry timers
- "Has Voted" indicator

### Flatmate Finder
Find compatible housemates:
- Student profiles with budget
- Preferred areas and move-in dates
- Interests and lifestyle
- Number of flatmates needed
- Direct messaging

### Ticket Resale
Student-to-student ticket marketplace:
- Event name and date
- Original and asking price
- Quantity available
- Seller information
- "Sold" status

### Sports Updates
Fixtures, results, and team news:
- Match previews and fixtures
- Live score updates
- Results and highlights
- Training schedule changes
- Team announcements

## Feed Intelligence

### Stage-Based Filtering

Content is automatically filtered based on your student journey stage:

**Exploring (Pre-Application):**
- Open days
- University comparisons
- Course information
- Application tips

**Applying:**
- UCAS updates
- Personal statement tips
- Offer day content
- Application deadlines

**Offer Holder:**
- Accommodation options
- Freshers prep content
- What to bring guides
- Course preparation

**Fresher (Year 1):**
- Freshers week events
- Making friends tips
- Society fairs
- Campus navigation
- Academic transition advice

**Returning Student:**
- Housing for next year
- Course-specific content
- Career opportunities
- Leadership positions in societies

<Note>
Your stage is automatically detected from your profile and updates as you progress through university.
</Note>

### Engagement Scoring

Posts are ranked using engagement signals:
- Likes and reactions
- Comments and replies
- Shares to groups
- Time since posting (recency)
- Relevance to your interests
- Your past engagement patterns

### Pinned Content

Important announcements can be pinned by:
- University administrators
- Student Union
- Society committee members
- Course coordinators

Pinned items appear at the top of the feed with an orange pin badge.

## Creating Posts

Tap the floating "+" button to create new content:

### Post Composer Options

**Post As:**
- Your personal account
- Any group where you're an admin
- Society accounts (if you're a committee member)

Change by tapping your profile picture in the composer header.

**Content Types:**
- Text post
- Photo post (with caption)
- Poll
- Event share

**Visibility:**
- Public: Visible to everyone at your university
- Group only: Visible to specific group members

**Additional Options:**
- Add location tag
- Tag other users
- Add emoji reactions

<Tip>
Posts with images get 3x more engagement than text-only posts.
</Tip>

## Interacting with Posts

### Likes
Tap the heart icon to like a post. Your like contributes to the post's engagement score.

### Comments
Tap the comment icon to:
- Add a comment
- View all comments
- Reply to existing comments
- React to comments

### Share to Group
Share interesting posts directly to relevant group chats. Perfect for:
- Sharing event info with your flat
- Posting society updates to course groups
- Forwarding accommodation deals

### Report
Report inappropriate content:
- Bullying or harassment
- Spam
- Misinformation
- Hate speech
- Explicit content

Reports are reviewed by moderators within 24 hours.

## Feed Customization

### Notification Preferences
Control what feed activity notifies you:
- Comments on your posts
- Replies to your comments
- Likes on your posts
- Posts from followed accounts
- Group post notifications

### Content Preferences
Adjust your feed algorithm:
- See more/less of specific content types
- Hide posts from specific groups
- Prioritize certain societies

## Empty States

If your feed is empty:

**For You Tab:**
> "Your feed is empty. Join groups and follow users to see personalized content here."

**Explore Tab:**
> "Nothing to explore yet. Check back soon for trending content."

**Following Tab:**
> "No posts from friends. Follow users and join groups to see their posts here."

<Tip>
Join a few groups and societies to start populating your feed with relevant content.
</Tip>

## Feed Header

The header shows:
- Menu button (access sidebar)
- University logo
- "Campus" title with stage subtitle
- Search button
- Notifications bell with unread badge

**Stage Subtitles:**
- "Finding Your Path" (Exploring)
- "Application Journey" (Applying)
- "Getting Ready" (Offer Holder)
- "Fresher Life" (First Year)
- "Year 2/3/4" (Returning)

## Pull to Refresh

Swipe down on the feed to refresh content and load new posts.

- New content loads 5 posts before reaching the end
