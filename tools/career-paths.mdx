Career Paths is Chapter's comprehensive career exploration tool that helps UK students discover careers that match their personality, compare different routes, and find real opportunities.

## Overview

Career Paths combines personality assessment, career data, and live job listings to help students make informed career decisions. The tool uses real salary data, graduate outcomes, and personalised matching to recommend careers.

## Key Features

### Career Discovery Hub

The main hub provides access to:

- **Browse Careers** - Explore 100+ careers across 12 categories including Technology, Healthcare, Creative & Media, Business & Finance, and more
- **Career Quiz** - RIASEC personality assessment that matches you with compatible careers
- **Search** - Find careers by name or category
- **Latest Results** - View your most recent quiz matches

### Career Quiz

The personality quiz uses the RIASEC model (Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, Conventional) to match students with suitable careers.

**How it works:**

1. Answer questions about your work preferences
2. Get a personality type code (e.g., ASE for Artistic-Social-Enterprising)
3. See ranked career matches with compatibility scores
4. View detailed explanations for each match

**Results include:**
- Top career matches with compatibility percentages
- Your dominant personality traits
- Match reasons explaining why each career suits you
- Ability to retake the quiz anytime

### Pathway Comparison

Compare different routes to the same career:

- **University Degree** - 3-4 year programmes with tuition costs
- **Apprenticeship** - Earn while you learn pathways
- **Degree Apprenticeship** - Combine work and university study
- **Direct Entry** - Start working immediately
- **Career Conversion** - Switch careers later in life

For each pathway, view:
- Total duration in months/years
- Cost breakdown (tuition, living costs)
- Milestones and stages
- Typical salary at each stage
- Pros and cons

### Live Job & Apprenticeship Listings

Find real opportunities through integration with UK job boards:

**Search filters:**
- Location and distance
- Salary range
- Apprenticeship level (Intermediate, Advanced, Higher, Degree)
- Contract type
- Apprenticeship route (Digital, Engineering, etc.)

**Listing details:**
- Job title and employer
- Salary information
- Location with distance from you
- Posted date
- Application deadline
- Direct link to apply

<Note>
Apprenticeships are tagged with the relevant career paths, making it easy to find opportunities in fields you're interested in.
</Note>

### Salary Maps

Visualise how salaries vary across UK regions:

- Interactive UK map showing regional pay differences
- Graduate salary data by location
- Compare earning potential in different cities
- Filter by career field

## Tips for Using Career Paths

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with the quiz">
    Take the personality quiz to get personalised career matches based on your interests and work style.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore multiple careers">
    Don't limit yourself - explore careers you hadn't considered. You might discover unexpected matches.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compare pathways">
    Look at different routes to the same career. An apprenticeship might be better value than university for some careers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check real salaries">
    Use the salary data to understand earning potential. Consider regional variations if you're flexible on location.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Shortlist opportunities">
    When you find interesting jobs or apprenticeships, add them to your lists for easy tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Salary data is based on UK averages and may vary by employer, location, and individual circumstances. Graduate salary figures are median values from official statistics.
</Warning>
