Career Arcade is Chapter's gamified career discovery platform featuring four interactive games that help you understand your career preferences through play rather than traditional assessments.

## Overview

Instead of filling out long questionnaires, Career Arcade lets you discover careers by playing quick, engaging games. Each game reveals different aspects of your work style, decision-making, and preferences - building a comprehensive career profile over time.

<Info>
All games contribute to your Career Arcade profile, which tracks your XP, level, and generates career insights based on your gameplay.
</Info>

## The Games

### Aim & Learn

**Test your career instincts**

Swipe through career-related statements and quickly decide if they appeal to you or not. Your rapid responses reveal genuine preferences.

**How it works:**
- Career statements appear on screen
- Swipe or tap to agree/disagree
- Build streaks for faster decisions
- Earn points based on speed and accuracy

**What it reveals:**
- Your gut reactions to different work scenarios
- Career fields that naturally appeal to you
- Work environment preferences
- Task preferences (hands-on vs analytical vs creative)

**Insights generated:**
- Career instincts profile
- Preferred work activities
- Top matching careers based on your responses

---

### Quick Think

**Word association challenge**

See a career-related word and type the first thing that comes to mind. Your associations reveal hidden preferences and thought patterns.

**How it works:**
- A word appears (e.g., "CAREER", "TEAM", "DEADLINE")
- Type your immediate association
- Faster responses earn more points
- AI analyses your word patterns

**What it reveals:**
- How you think about work concepts
- Your associations with different career aspects
- Communication style
- Values and priorities

**Insights generated:**
- Work style tags (e.g., "collaborative", "independent", "creative")
- Career fields aligned with your thinking patterns
- Communication preferences

<Note>
Quick Think uses AI to analyse your word associations and identify patterns. All responses are private and used only to improve your career recommendations.
</Note>

---

### Day in the Life

**90 seconds. Endless decisions.**

Experience rapid-fire workplace scenarios across different careers. Sort tasks by priority and see how you handle pressure.

**How it works:**
- Tasks appear representing real work situations
- Swipe in four directions based on urgency and importance:
  - **Up** - Urgent and Important (do now)
  - **Right** - Important but not urgent (schedule)
  - **Left** - Urgent but not important (delegate)
  - **Down** - Neither urgent nor important (eliminate)
- 90 seconds per round
- Multiple career environments to try

**Career environments available:**
- Software Engineer
- Marketing Manager  
- Teacher
- Healthcare Professional
- Financial Analyst
- And more

**What it reveals:**
- Decision-making speed under pressure
- Prioritisation skills
- Stress tolerance
- Which work environments energise vs drain you
- Leadership vs individual contributor preferences

**Insights generated:**
- Best career environments for your style
- Decision-making profile
- Stress tolerance score
- Task prioritisation accuracy

<Tip>
Try multiple career environments to discover which types of work decisions feel natural vs stressful. This helps identify careers where you'll thrive.
</Tip>

### Career Insights

Based on your gameplay, the system generates:

- **Leadership Score** - Tendency to take charge vs follow
- **Solo Preference** - Independent vs collaborative work preference  
- **Stress Tolerance** - How you handle pressure
- **Decision Speed** - Fast vs deliberate decision-making
- **Best Environments** - Which career settings suit you
- **Work Style Tags** - Descriptors like "analytical", "creative", "detail-oriented"
- **Top Career Matches** - Careers aligned with your profile

<Info>
Insights improve as you play more games. Playing all three active games gives the most accurate profile.
</Info>

## Scoring

### Aim & Learn
- Base points per correct swipe: 10 points
- Speed bonus: Up to 5 extra points for fast decisions
- Streak multiplier: 2x at 5 streak, 3x at 10 streak

### Quick Think  
- Base points per response: 15 points
- Speed bonus: Up to 10 extra points (faster = more points)
- Quality bonus: AI rates response relevance (+5 points)

### Day in the Life
- Correct prioritisation: 20 points
- Speed bonus: Up to 10 extra points
- Perfect round bonus: 100 points for all correct
- Streak bonus: 5 points per consecutive correct decision

- Power the leaderboard system

Your responses are never shared publicly. Only your XP total and username appear on leaderboards.
